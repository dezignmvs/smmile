SuJok therapy

SuJok therapy is a wonderful, drugless Korean therapy invented by Prof. Park Jae Woo, an eminent philosopher and scientist. According to Sujok, our body is represented in miniature form on our hands & feet which is the remote control to influence our whole body and cure diseases. The treatments aim is to balance the imbalanced energy with simple tools like seeds, colors, magnets, needles, moxibustion thus harmonizing the energy on physical, emotional and mental levels.

Acupuncture

Acupuncture is an ancient healing science based on the theory that the body’s vital force, energy or qi flows along invisible pathways called meridians. Disease occurs when there is a blockage of energy or qi in these meridians. Acupuncture therapy is a method of releasing the blocked qi with the help of needles, acupressure, moxibustion, cupping and electric current.
